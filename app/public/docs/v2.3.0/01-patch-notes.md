**Rename NPR**

An “SM Race Title” button has been added to the Intelligence and Foreign Relations window. This allows you to change the actual name of the selected alien race, rather than just the name you assign to them.

Pressing the button results in two prompts for information. The first is for the NPR Species Name, which will also be used as the race short name by the NPR. The second is the NPR Race Title, which will also be the new name for the alien race on the Intelligence window.

As you may make the change before contact is established, the real name of the NPR Species and Title is populated in the popup boxes. You can change or leave as it is.
